          💡 What is VelocityIQ

          VelocityIQ is an AI-powered reconditioning and inventory intelligence add-on for Solera's Inventory+ platform. It solves the three biggest money leaks in used car operations — slow recon, missed customer connections, and blind daily decisions — in one $299/month subscription.

          🤖 How the AI Works
          🔧

          Work Type Selected

          Sets the base estimate. 70% weight

          📊

          Vendor Historical Avg

          Adjusts up or down from base. 15% weight

          📦

          Vendor Queue Load

          Adds buffer if the vendor is overloaded. 8% weight

          🚙

          Vehicle Type & Condition

          Luxury and high-mileage units take longer.

          🕐

          Day of Week & Scan-in Time

          Weekends and off-hours add delay.

          🧠

          Dealership-Specific Pattern

          The model learns this dealer's actual pace over time.

          Predicted Date = Base (work type) + Vendor adjustment + Queue buffer + Day offset
          Worked example → Unit 4821 · Full Recon · Monday 10am · AutoMech assigned → Predicted: Friday Jun 13 (5.8 days)
          🔧

          Recon Velocity

          Live 5-stage tracking with per-second floorplan cost. Cuts days out of recon so capital stops bleeding.

          🤝

          Customer Match

          The moment a unit is Ready, AI matches it to real CRM shoppers. No more finished cars sitting unseen.

          🧠

          GM Briefing

          A daily AI-written brief on what needs action today. Replaces blind decisions with a clear morning plan.
